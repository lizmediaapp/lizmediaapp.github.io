How to play music from Dropbox on Android

LizMusic can connect to your own Dropbox account so you can play personal audio files stored there, including lawful music files, recordings, practice tracks, or karaoke recordings.

Dropbox and copyright boundary

Dropbox is a trademark of Dropbox, Inc. LizMusic is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Dropbox. LizMusic does not provide music, shared libraries, download links, or copyright bypass tools.

Setup flow

  1. Put lawful personal audio files in your own Dropbox folder.
  2. Open LizMusic and add Dropbox as a storage source.
  3. Authorize with your own Dropbox account.
  4. Select a folder and refresh the library.
  5. Play or cache only files you are allowed to use.
