Google Drive Music

Use LizMusic as a Google Drive music player for Android

LizMusic can work with your own Google Drive music library so you can keep files in cloud storage while using an Android player built for listening, organization, and personal-library workflows.

What to know before using Google Drive

Use your own account

Google Drive access uses the account and folders you authorize. LizMusic does not provide hosted music storage.

Keep files organized

A clear folder structure makes browsing, playback, and troubleshooting easier inside any cloud-backed library.

Plan for weak networks

Offline-friendly workflows are still useful when cloud access is slow, unavailable, or inconsistent.
