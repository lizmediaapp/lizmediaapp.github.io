5.1 How to Cancel: Cancel through your Google Play account settings > Subscriptions > LizMusic Sponsorship. You cannot cancel directly through the LizMusic app.

5.2 Timing: You must cancel at least 24 hours before your next billing date to avoid being charged for the next period.

5.4 After Cancellation: You keep all benefits (including ad-free access) until the end of your paid period. After that, you will lose ad-free access and cannot sponsor again.
