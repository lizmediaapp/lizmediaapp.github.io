Local Music

Use LizMusic as a local music player for Android

LizMusic is built for people who keep their own music files. You can use it for local playback on Android, then extend the same library workflow with WebDAV, OneDrive, offline cache, playlists, and lyrics.

Your files stay yours

LizMusic is a player client. It is designed around music you already own, organize, and choose to play.

Offline-friendly

Local playback helps when you travel, commute, or listen in places where network quality is unpredictable.

Library tools

Playlists, favorites, album browsing, and local lyrics files help make personal collections easier to use.
