Personal Audio

Build a personal audio library on Android

LizMusic is designed for personal audio files you own, created yourself, recorded yourself, or have permission to use. That can include legally obtained music, personal recordings, practice tracks, and karaoke recordings.

Copyright and content boundary

LizMusic does not provide music, karaoke tracks, backing tracks, shared libraries, download links, or tools for bypassing copyright restrictions. You are responsible for ensuring that any audio files you play are owned by you, created by you, recorded by you, or used with permission.

Local files

Keep albums, recordings, and practice audio directly on your Android device.

Cloud storage

Connect your own Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or WebDAV storage for files you control.

Offline listening

Use offline-friendly workflows for lawful audio files you need when connectivity is weak.
