All LizMusic Features

A WebDAV music player for Android that fits self-hosted libraries.

LizMusic supports WebDAV-compatible servers so you can play music from your own NAS, home server, or hosted storage instead of moving your collection into a streaming platform. The Android app is built for people who already manage their own files and want a dedicated player, not a generic file browser.

Why WebDAV support matters

WebDAV is common in self-hosted storage stacks because it works across NAS devices, private cloud tools, and hosted servers. LizMusic uses that connection model as a real playback source, which makes it useful if your albums already live outside the phone but still belong to you.

  • Connect your own WebDAV-compatible music folders
  • Browse and play your library from one Android app
  • Keep playlists and listening workflows inside a music-first interface

What LizMusic adds beyond basic file access

  • Playlist and favorites management for large personal collections
  • Lyrics support and album-focused browsing
  • Offline cache workflows when a network connection is unreliable
  • Bluetooth and car-friendly playback controls for daily listening
