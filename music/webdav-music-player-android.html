WebDAV Playback

Use LizMusic as a WebDAV music player for Android

LizMusic supports WebDAV-compatible servers so you can play music from your own NAS, home server, or hosted storage without moving your collection into a streaming platform.

Why this page exists

This page is for listeners who already manage their own files and want a dedicated Android playback experience instead of a generic file browser.

Self-hosted libraries

WebDAV is common in NAS and private-cloud setups, so LizMusic fits people who want to keep control of their albums.

Music-first workflow

Instead of browsing folders manually, you get a player workflow with playlists, favorites, and browsing that feels built for listening.

Offline-friendly follow-up

If your network is unstable, the app also supports offline cache workflows so your library is not locked behind perfect connectivity.
