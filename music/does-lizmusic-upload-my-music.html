Privacy FAQ

Does LizMusic upload my music?

LizMusic is designed as a player for local files and storage sources you authorize. It does not provide music content, public hosting, shared libraries, download links, or tools for bypassing copyright restrictions.

Use personal audio files only

You are responsible for ensuring that any audio files you play are owned by you, created by you, recorded by you, or used with permission.

Local files

Local audio remains on your device unless you choose a separate storage workflow.

Cloud sources

Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and WebDAV are accounts or servers you authorize.

Provider controls

You can disconnect storage or revoke provider access using provider account settings.
