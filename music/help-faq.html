Storage

Does LizMusic provide free WebDAV servers?

No. LizMusic is a music player client. You connect it to your own WebDAV-compatible storage or server.

Do I need to create my own OneDrive account?

Yes. If you want OneDrive playback, you need your own Microsoft account and your own OneDrive storage.

Can I use the app without cloud storage?

Yes. LizMusic supports local music playback on Android devices without requiring WebDAV or OneDrive.

Who manages the server-side or account-side setup?

You do. LizMusic handles playback and library access on the client side, while the underlying server or storage account remains your responsibility.

Why does LizMusic ask for storage or media access permission?

Local playback depends on Android permission to read your audio files, artwork, and related media metadata. Without that access, the app cannot build or refresh your local library correctly.

Can I switch between local, WebDAV, and OneDrive storage later?

Yes. You can add, remove, or change storage locations in the app settings. The supported path depends on the storage type you configure.

Library

My music files do not show up. What should I check first?

Start with permissions, then confirm the files are supported audio formats, then refresh or rescan the library. If you are using cloud storage, verify the target folder really contains the music files you expect.

How do I add new music to the app?

Add the files to your device or supported storage first, then refresh or rescan in LizMusic. For cloud storage, make sure the newly added files are inside the folder path your storage configuration actually uses.

Why do I see duplicate songs?

Duplicate tracks usually mean the same files are being discovered from more than one storage path, or the same folder was added twice. Review your configured storages and remove overlapping sources.

Why is album art missing or incorrect?

Cover art usually depends on embedded metadata or image files that match your library organization. If metadata is incomplete or inconsistent, LizMusic may show missing or unexpected artwork until the library is rescanned.

Can LizMusic work with local lyrics files and playlists?

Yes. LizMusic supports local-library listening features such as playlists, favorites, and local lyrics workflows alongside supported storage types.

Playback

Why is there no sound when I press play?

Check device volume, Bluetooth routing, and whether another app is taking audio focus. If the file opens but stays silent, try another track to rule out file-specific issues.

Why does playback stop after the screen turns off?

Many Android devices limit background activity aggressively. Review battery optimization and background playback permissions for LizMusic if playback stops when the app is no longer foregrounded.

How does Bluetooth playback work?

Pair the Bluetooth device through Android first, then start playback in LizMusic. Audio output and many basic controls should follow the system-level Bluetooth connection.

What if OneDrive playback suddenly fails?

The most common causes are expired authorization, the wrong Microsoft account, or a file that was moved or deleted. Reconnecting the OneDrive storage is often the first recovery step.

What if WebDAV playback is slow or fails intermittently?

That usually points to network quality, server performance, or remote permissions rather than the player UI itself. Test the same server path from another trusted client to confirm whether the issue is server-side.

Does the app support offline-friendly listening?

Yes. Local playback works offline by design, and cloud-backed listening can still benefit from offline cache or already-downloaded files depending on your workflow.

Why is the playback notification missing?

Check both LizMusic notification settings and Android system notification permission settings. On some devices, lock-screen visibility or battery restrictions can also hide or interrupt the playback notification.

Why do headphone or Bluetooth control buttons not respond?

Confirm the external device is connected correctly and review the playback or external-device related settings in the app. Some accessories also depend on device-specific button mappings outside LizMusic.

Does LizMusic include a sleep timer?

Yes. LizMusic includes a sleep-timer workflow in the player controls so playback can stop automatically after a selected time.

Does the app support equalizer or audio effects?

LizMusic can work with the device audio-effects path when the Android device provides one. The exact equalizer behavior depends on the device and system audio stack.

Privacy and support

How does LizMusic handle my data?

LizMusic is a player client. Your music remains in your own local storage or the third-party storage accounts you connect. For the broader privacy picture, refer to the published privacy policy.

What should I send when I report a bug?

Tell us the storage type, the device, the action you were taking, and the exact error message or a screenshot if possible. That context usually shortens support time significantly.

When should I contact the storage provider instead of LizMusic?

If the issue is account recovery, billing, password reset, server provisioning, or service availability, contact the underlying provider. LizMusic handles the playback client side, not the external service itself.

Can support give me a server or account to test with?

No. LizMusic support does not distribute public WebDAV servers, shared accounts, or third-party music libraries. Troubleshooting focuses on your own setup.

Why does the app seem to use more battery during long sessions?

Battery use can increase with long screen-on sessions, Bluetooth playback, cloud-backed streaming, or aggressive rescanning. Local playback and already-cached files are usually the lightest path for extended listening.

What if the app crashes repeatedly?

Try clearing cache, checking available storage, restarting the device, and making sure you are on a current build. If the crash continues, send support the failing action, device model, and any error details you can capture.

Need more than a quick answer?

Use the dedicated setup guides when you want step-by-step help, and use the support page when you already know the exact failure point.
