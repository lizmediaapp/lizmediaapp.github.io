Feedback and Support

Tell us what happened so we can help faster

LizMusic support works best when you include the storage type you were using, what step failed, and any visible error text. If your issue is tied to WebDAV or OneDrive, mention that clearly in the subject or first sentence.

Contact email

For general support, bug reports, and feature ideas, email us at:

lizmediaapp@gmail.com

Good subject lines

  • Bug Report: WebDAV playback fails after saving storage
  • Support: OneDrive authorization loop
  • Feedback: local library scanning improvement

What to include in your message

Device and Android version

Tell us what phone or tablet you use and which Android version it is running.

Storage type

Say whether the issue came from local files, WebDAV, or OneDrive.

What you expected

Describe the action you were taking and what result you expected to see.

Error text or screenshots

If you can capture an error message or screenshot, include it. That usually shortens the back-and-forth a lot.

Before you email us

  • Check the relevant WebDAV or OneDrive guide first.
  • Make sure the storage account or server still works in another trusted client.
  • If the problem is server-side or account-side, contact that provider as well.
