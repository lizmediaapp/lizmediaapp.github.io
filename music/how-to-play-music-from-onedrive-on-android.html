OneDrive Playback

How to play music from OneDrive on Android

LizMusic can connect to your own OneDrive account so you can play personal audio files stored there, including lawful music files, recordings, practice tracks, or karaoke recordings.

OneDrive and copyright boundary

Microsoft OneDrive is a trademark of Microsoft. LizMusic is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Microsoft. LizMusic does not provide music, shared libraries, download links, or copyright bypass tools.

Setup flow

  1. Put lawful personal audio files in your own OneDrive folder.
  2. Open LizMusic and add OneDrive as a storage source.
  3. Authorize with your own Microsoft account.
  4. Select a folder and refresh the library.
  5. Play or cache only files you are allowed to use.
