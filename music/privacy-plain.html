Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to LizMusic. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our music player application.

IMPORTANT: LizMusic is a music player client application only. We do NOT provide, host, store, stream, or distribute any music content. You must provide your own music files and cloud storage accounts.

2. Data We Collect

When you use LizMusic, we may collect the following information:

  • Music Library Metadata: Information about your music files' metadata (artist, album, title, duration) - stored locally on YOUR device only
  • Playback History: Tracks you've played, playlists, and listening preferences (stored locally on your device, NEVER uploaded to our servers)
  • Cloud Storage Credentials: If you choose to use cloud integration (WebDAV, OneDrive, Google Drive, Dropbox), encrypted connection credentials are stored locally on your device
  • App Settings: Your preferences, equalizer settings, customization choices, and feature toggles
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, audio output device type for compatibility purposes
  • Analytics and Diagnostics: Anonymous usage statistics, crash reports, and performance metrics collected via Firebase (Google) to improve app stability and user experience
  • Sensor Data: Accelerometer data when shake-to-skip feature is enabled (processed locally on your device, not uploaded)
  • Bluetooth Information: Connected bluetooth device names and types when using car mode or bluetooth playback features
  • Subscription Information: Purchase records and subscription status for premium features (processed by Google Play)

IMPORTANT: We do NOT collect, access, or store your actual music files or audio content. Your music remains on your device or in your personal cloud storage accounts. We only process metadata for playback purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the music playback functionality
  • Sync your music library across your devices (if you enable cloud features)
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience through analytics
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and bugs through automated error reporting
  • Monitor app performance and optimize resource usage
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Manage subscriptions and process in-app purchases
  • Deliver app updates and new features

4. Privacy-First Approach

LizMusic is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Local Storage: All your music data, playback history, and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Limited Analytics: We collect only anonymous usage data and crash reports via Firebase to improve app quality. We do not track your specific music listening content or build detailed user profiles
  • No Account Required: You can use LizMusic without creating an account or providing personal information
  • Encrypted Credentials: Cloud storage credentials are encrypted and stored only on your device
  • No Data Selling: We will never sell your personal data to third parties
  • Minimal Permissions: We only request permissions that are essential for specific features you choose to use
  • Transparent Tracking: All data collection is disclosed in this policy, and most analytics features help us fix bugs and crashes

5. Cloud Storage Integration

User-Provided Cloud Storage: LizMusic does NOT provide cloud storage services. To use cloud sync features, you must have your own accounts with third-party providers (WebDAV servers, OneDrive, Google Drive, Dropbox) that YOU set up and pay for independently.

When you connect your personal cloud storage accounts, your music files are synced directly between YOUR device and YOUR cloud provider. LizMusic does NOT store, access, or process your music files on our servers. We only store the encrypted connection credentials locally on your device.

Your cloud provider's privacy policy applies to any data stored in your cloud accounts. You are responsible for:

  • Signing up for and paying for cloud storage subscriptions
  • Maintaining the security of your cloud storage credentials
  • Ensuring you have legal rights to all content in your cloud storage
  • Complying with your cloud provider's terms and privacy policies

LizMusic has no access to, control over, or responsibility for the content in your cloud storage accounts.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

LizMusic integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Analytics, crash reporting (Crashlytics), performance monitoring, and remote configuration
  • Google Play Services: In-app updates, subscription billing, and payment processing
  • Cloud Storage Providers: Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and WebDAV-compatible servers for file synchronization

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to read them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. Data shared with these services is governed by their respective policies.

8. Children's Privacy

LizMusic does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your playlists and settings
  • Opt-out of any optional data collection

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Permissions Explained

LizMusic requests the following permissions to provide its features. All permissions are used only for their stated purpose:

  • Storage/Media Access: To read and play your music files from device storage
  • Internet Access: For cloud storage synchronization, app updates, and analytics
  • Bluetooth: To connect to car audio systems and wireless speakers for playback control
  • Notifications: To display playback controls and now-playing information
  • Run at Startup: Optional feature to restore playback state when device reboots
  • Display over Other Apps: Optional floating player widget for quick access while using other apps
  • Motion Sensors: Optional shake-to-skip feature using device accelerometer (data processed locally)
  • Prevent Device from Sleeping: To maintain playback during long sessions

You can revoke any permission at any time through your device settings. Some features may not work if related permissions are denied.
