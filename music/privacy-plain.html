1. Introduction

Welcome to LizMusic. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our music player application.

IMPORTANT: LizMusic is a music player client application only. We do NOT provide, host, store, stream, or distribute any music content. You must provide your own music files and cloud storage accounts.

2. Data We Collect and Access

When you use LizMusic, we may access or collect the following information:

Data Accessed Locally (NOT Uploaded to Servers)

  • Music Files and Audio Files: We access your music files (MP3, FLAC, AAC, etc.) and other audio files stored on your device or cloud storage for playback purposes only. These files remain on YOUR device or YOUR cloud storage and are NEVER uploaded to our servers
  • Album Artwork and Embedded Images: We access album cover images embedded in your music files or stored as separate image files (JPG, PNG) in your music folders for display purposes. These images are processed locally and not uploaded
  • Music Library Metadata: Information extracted from your music files including artist, album, title, duration, genre, and other ID3 tags - stored locally on YOUR device only
  • Files and Documents (Music-Related Only): We may access playlist files (.m3u, .pls), lyrics files (.lrc), and music-related documents in your music folders. We do NOT access or process your personal documents, photos unrelated to music, videos, or other non-music files
  • Playback History: Tracks you've played, playlists, and listening preferences (stored locally on your device, NEVER uploaded to our servers)
  • Cloud Storage Credentials and Authorization Tokens: If you choose to use cloud integration such as Google Drive, WebDAV-compatible servers, or OneDrive, connection credentials, refresh tokens, or equivalent authorization data may be stored locally on your device in encrypted form to maintain your chosen connection
  • App Settings: Your preferences, equalizer settings, customization choices, and feature toggles

Data Collected/Transmitted

  • Pseudonymous Installation and Analytics Identifiers: Firebase services may assign installation-scoped or pseudonymous identifiers such as Firebase Installation ID and Firebase Analytics identifiers to support analytics, diagnostics, remote configuration, and limited-release feature controls. These identifiers are not your real name or email address
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version, audio output device type for compatibility purposes
  • Analytics and Diagnostics: Usage statistics, crash reports, and performance metrics may be collected via Firebase (Google) to improve app stability and user experience. We do NOT upload your music files, and we do NOT intentionally send full listening-history content as part of analytics events
  • Ads and Consent Data: If advertising is enabled in your app build or region, Google Mobile Ads and Google User Messaging Platform may process device, app, network, and consent-related information, and may use advertising identifiers where supported by Android and permitted by your device settings and Google policies
  • Network and Connectivity Information: Network connection type and related diagnostic signals may be processed to improve reliability, playback compatibility, and ad or analytics delivery
  • Optional Lyrics Lookup Metadata: If a limited-release lyrics lookup feature is enabled for your installation and you choose to use it, song metadata such as title, artist, album, or duration may be sent to a third-party lyrics service to try to match lyrics. Any lyrics saved from that feature are cached on your device
  • Bluetooth Information: Connected bluetooth device names and types when using car mode or bluetooth playback features
  • Subscription Information: Purchase records and subscription status for premium features (processed by Google Play)

Important Clarifications

  • Music Files: We access and play your music files locally. Your music files are NEVER uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties
  • Lyrics Files: LizMusic can read user-provided local lyrics files such as .lrc files stored with your music library. These files remain on your device or your own storage accounts unless you separately choose to use a limited-release lyrics lookup feature
  • Photos and Videos: While our storage permission may technically allow access to photos and videos on older Android versions (due to READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission limitations), we do NOT access, process, collect, or view your personal photos or videos. We only access music-related images (album artwork)
  • Audio Recordings: We do NOT record audio or access your voice recordings, voice memos, or other recorded content. We only access pre-existing music and audio files that you have stored for playback
  • Documents: We do NOT access your personal documents, PDFs, text files, or other non-music documents. File access is limited to music files and music-related metadata only

CRITICAL PRIVACY NOTICE: Your music files, audio content, and personal media NEVER leave your device or personal cloud storage. We are a local music player - core music playback happens on your device. Network traffic is limited to app infrastructure and optional services you choose to use, such as analytics, ads, cloud connections, updates, or limited-release lyrics lookup.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the music playback functionality
  • Sync your music library using your own connected cloud storage providers (if you enable cloud features)
  • Apply remote configuration, device-specific feature flags, and rollout controls
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience through analytics
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and bugs through automated error reporting
  • Monitor app performance and optimize resource usage
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Manage subscriptions and process in-app purchases
  • Request and store advertising consent where required, and serve ads when enabled
  • Deliver app updates and new features

3a. Google Drive Authorization and Data Access

If you choose to connect Google Drive, LizMusic requests access only after you explicitly authorize the app with your own Google account. For the first public Google Drive release, LizMusic is designed to work with a folder that you choose manually from your own My Drive account.

Once authorized, LizMusic may read the names, paths, file metadata, and file contents of audio files and related artwork within the Google Drive scope you approve in order to browse your library, import tracks, prepare playback, cache media for offline use, and reconnect to the same folder later.

LizMusic does not upload your Google Drive music files to LizMusic-operated servers, does not resell or redistribute those files, and does not use Google Drive data to build an advertising profile. Google account authorization can be revoked at any time through LizMusic, your Google account permissions, or by disconnecting the storage source inside the app.

LizMusic has no ongoing custody of, control over, or responsibility for the content in your cloud storage accounts beyond the client-side access you explicitly authorize for playback features.

11. Permissions Explained

LizMusic requests the following permissions to provide its features. All permissions are used only for their stated purpose:

  • Internet Access (INTERNET): Required for cloud storage synchronization with your own cloud accounts, app updates, subscriptions, optional analytics, optional ads, and limited-release online services
  • Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): Used to determine connectivity status for playback reliability, cloud access, analytics, ad loading, and update behavior
  • Storage/Media Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_AUDIO): To read and play your music files from device storage. On Android 13+, we use READ_MEDIA_AUDIO for more granular media-only access
  • Storage Write Access (WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Used only on Android 9 and below to save playlists and app data to external storage
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK): To keep music playing in the background and display persistent playback notifications
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): To display playback controls, now-playing information, and media notifications (Android 13+ requires explicit permission)
  • Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK): To prevent the device from sleeping during active playback sessions
  • Bluetooth (BLUETOOTH, BLUETOOTH_CONNECT): To connect to car audio systems, wireless headphones, and speakers for playback control and audio output
  • Boot Completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): Optional feature to restore playback state or resume widgets when device reboots

You can revoke any permission at any time through your device settings (Settings 鈫?Apps 鈫?LizMusic 鈫?Permissions). Some features may not work if related permissions are denied.
