Local vs Cloud

Local files, cloud music, or both on Android?

LizMusic supports local folders, Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, WebDAV, and offline-friendly listening so you can pick the storage model that fits your own library.

Use local files when

  • You want playback without account authorization.
  • You often listen without reliable internet.
  • Your Android device has enough storage for the albums you use most.

Use cloud music when

  • Your full library already lives in Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or WebDAV.
  • You want access without copying every album to the phone.
  • You manage files from multiple devices.

Use a hybrid setup when

  • You keep the full library in cloud storage.
  • You cache favorites for travel or weak networks.
  • You want one player for both local and remote music.
