Local Lyrics and Library Tools

Use LizMusic as an Android music player with local lyrics files, playlists, and library-first listening

LizMusic is not just about connecting storage. It also gives your personal collection a music-focused listening workflow with support for your own local lyrics files, playlists, favorites, and album or artist browsing.

Why this matters for music-library users

Local lyrics support

Local lyrics files can turn a file collection into a more engaging listening experience, especially when you already own the music and want a richer player workflow.

Playlists and favorites

Once your library grows, organization matters just as much as playback. LizMusic is built around both.

Works across storage types

The listening tools still matter whether your music comes from local files, WebDAV servers, or OneDrive-backed libraries.
