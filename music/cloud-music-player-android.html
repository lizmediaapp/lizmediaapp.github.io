Cloud Music

A cloud music player for Android libraries you control

LizMusic connects local files, Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and WebDAV into one music-player experience for Android. It is built for personal audio files you own, created, recorded, or have permission to use.

Personal audio files only

LizMusic does not provide music, karaoke tracks, backing tracks, shared libraries, download links, or tools for bypassing copyright restrictions. Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, and WebDAV are storage locations you configure or authorize.

Google Drive

Play audio files stored in your own Google Drive account.

Dropbox

Use LizMusic as a Dropbox music player for your own folders.

OneDrive

Connect Microsoft OneDrive music files to Android playback.

WebDAV

Use NAS, self-hosted, or WebDAV-compatible music libraries.

Choose the right listening workflow

Cloud first

Good when your music library already lives in Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or WebDAV and you want access across devices.

Local first

Best for predictable playback, no sign-in flow, and music stored directly on the Android device.

Hybrid

Use cloud storage for your full library and offline cache for albums you need when connectivity drops.
