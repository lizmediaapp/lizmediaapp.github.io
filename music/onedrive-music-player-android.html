OneDrive Playback

Use LizMusic as a OneDrive music player for Android

LizMusic includes OneDrive playback support for people who already store albums, singles, or archive folders in Microsoft cloud storage and want a proper Android listening workflow.

Who this page is for

Cloud-first libraries

If your personal collection already lives in OneDrive, LizMusic gives it a player workflow instead of a generic cloud-file experience.

Organized listening

The app combines playback with playlists, favorites, lyrics, and browsing that still feels music-first after your library grows.

Offline follow-up

If you do not want your library tied to a perfect connection, LizMusic also supports offline-friendly listening paths.
