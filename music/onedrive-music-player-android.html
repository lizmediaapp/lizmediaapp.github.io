An Android music player for OneDrive-based music libraries.

LizMusic includes OneDrive playback support for people who already store albums, singles, or archive folders in Microsoft cloud storage. Instead of treating OneDrive as a generic document bucket, the app brings those files into an Android music-player workflow with library management and listening controls.

Best fit for listeners who already use OneDrive

If your personal music library lives in OneDrive, LizMusic gives you a more focused playback layer than browsing files manually. The Android project includes dedicated OneDrive authorization and cloud-backed playback support so the feature is not just marketing text on the website.

Useful companion features

  • Playlist management for albums, mixes, and favorites
  • Lyrics support inside the player workflow
  • Offline cache options for weak-network listening
  • Bluetooth and car-focused playback settings on Android
