Dropbox Playback

Use LizMusic as a Dropbox music player for Android

LizMusic helps you play audio files stored in your own Dropbox account while keeping the same Android music-player workflow you use for local files, Google Drive, OneDrive, and WebDAV.

Why Dropbox support matters

Bring your own files

LizMusic is a player client. Your music stays in the Dropbox account and folders that you control.

Music-first browsing

Use a dedicated playback interface instead of treating albums like generic files in a cloud-drive browser.

Offline follow-up

Pair cloud playback with offline-friendly listening when network quality is not reliable.
