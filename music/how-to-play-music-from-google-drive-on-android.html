Google Drive Playback

How to play music from Google Drive on Android

LizMusic can connect to your own Google Drive account so you can play personal audio files stored there, including lawful music files, recordings, practice tracks, or karaoke recordings.

Google Drive and copyright boundary

Google Drive is a trademark of Google. LizMusic is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Google. LizMusic does not provide music, shared libraries, download links, or copyright bypass tools.

Setup flow

  1. Put lawful personal audio files in your own Google Drive.
  2. Open LizMusic and add Google Drive as a storage source.
  3. Authorize with your own Google account.
  4. Select a folder and refresh the library.
  5. Play or cache only files you are allowed to use.
