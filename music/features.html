LizMusic Home

Feature Overview

LizMusic is built for people who manage their own music library.

The Android app combines local playback, cloud-connected libraries, offline access, synced lyrics, playlists, car-friendly controls, and equalizer tools in one player. It is designed for listeners who want control over their files instead of renting a streaming catalog.

Connected Music Sources

LizMusic supports local device storage, WebDAV-compatible servers, and OneDrive. That makes it a fit for self-hosted NAS libraries, private cloud folders, and classic offline collections stored directly on Android.

  • Play local music files from your phone or tablet
  • Browse WebDAV servers and stream your own library
  • Use OneDrive as a remote music source on Android

Listening Tools

Beyond playback, the app includes offline cache flows, playlists, favorites, lyrics support, audio effects, Bluetooth behavior settings, and car-oriented playback options for everyday use.

  • Offline cache and rule-based downloads
  • Lyrics support and organized playlists
  • Equalizer controls, Bluetooth auto-play, and shake-to-skip
