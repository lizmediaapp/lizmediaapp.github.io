WebDAV Playback

How to play audio from WebDAV on Android

To play audio from WebDAV on Android, connect your own WebDAV server, choose a folder, and use LizMusic to browse files you own, created, recorded, or have permission to use.

No content is provided by LizMusic

LizMusic does not provide music, karaoke tracks, backing tracks, shared libraries, download links, or copyright bypass tools.

Playback steps

  1. Confirm the WebDAV server is reachable.
  2. Use HTTPS and your private login when available.
  3. Add WebDAV in LizMusic storage settings.
  4. Select your folder of lawful personal audio files.
  5. Play, organize, and cache only files you are allowed to use.
