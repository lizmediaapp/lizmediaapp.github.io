Privacy

Is cloud music playback private?

Cloud playback privacy depends on the app, the provider, and the permissions you grant. LizMusic is designed to connect to accounts and servers you authorize for browsing and playback of your personal audio files.

Personal files and provider rules

Use only audio files you own, created, recorded, or have permission to use. Third-party providers keep their own privacy, security, copyright, and account rules.

Authorization

Cloud providers ask you to authorize access with your own account.

Disconnection

You can disconnect a storage source or revoke access from provider settings.

No content source

LizMusic is a player and does not provide music or shared libraries.
