About LizSpeedTest

Fast, Simple, Reliable

Who We Are

LizSpeedTest is developed and maintained by Liz Media, an independent software team dedicated to building lightweight, user-friendly tools for everyday mobile users. We believe measuring your internet speed should be instant, accurate, and easy to understand.

Our mission is simple: give you a clear, honest picture of your network performance without unnecessary complexity or intrusive data collection.

What LizSpeedTest Does

  • Download & Upload Speed

    Accurately measures your real-world download and upload bandwidth using distributed test servers.

  • Ping & Latency

    Reports round-trip latency so you can evaluate connection quality for gaming, video calls, and streaming.

  • Test History

    Keep a local log of every test so you can track how your connection changes over time.

  • Server Selection

    Automatically selects the nearest available server, or lets you manually choose a preferred location.

  • Platform Availability

    Available now on Android, with additional platforms planned for future releases.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We do not sell your personal data. Test results are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. We use Firebase Analytics to understand aggregate app usage patterns, Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash reports, and Firebase Performance Monitoring to improve reliability.

For the full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
