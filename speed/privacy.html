Privacy at a glance

  • Stored locally: Speed test history, timestamps, server information, and selected app settings are kept on your device.
  • Shared during testing: Your device connects to speed test servers and necessarily sends technical connection data such as your public IP address.
  • Third-party services: LizSpeedTest uses Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Performance Monitoring for aggregate analytics, reliability, and performance diagnostics.
  • No sale of personal data: We do not sell your personal data and we do not use it for targeted advertising.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Liz Media handles information related to the LizSpeedTest Android application. By downloading or using the app, you agree to the data practices described here.

2. Information stored on your device

LizSpeedTest stores core speed test information locally on your device so you can review results over time.

  • Speed test results: download speed, upload speed, ping, jitter, and packet loss.
  • Test context: server name, server city, date, and time of each completed test.
  • Network context: public IP address and ISP name returned by the speed test service at the time of testing.
  • Wi-Fi metadata: SSID, band, signal strength, and channel when available during a Wi-Fi test.
  • App settings: language, units, connection mode, speed scale, and history preferences.

This local information is not uploaded to Liz Media servers as part of normal app use.

3. Information transmitted during speed tests

When you run a speed test, the app communicates with third-party speed test infrastructure. This is necessary for the app to function.

  • Your public IP address is visible to the test server as part of a normal internet connection.
  • Download and upload payloads are exchanged solely to measure connection performance.
  • The app may request the available server list and approximate network region from the speed test service.

4. Background testing and notifications

If you enable automatic testing, LizSpeedTest may run scheduled background tests and send result notifications. You can disable this at any time in the app settings, and you can revoke notification permission in Android system settings.

5. Third-party services

LizSpeedTest uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: anonymous aggregate app usage information.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: crash reports used to diagnose and fix bugs.
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: anonymous performance metrics used to improve app responsiveness and stability.

These services are governed by Google policies. Learn more at Google Privacy Policy.

6. What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising.
  • We do not request contacts, camera, microphone, or file access for core app functionality.
  • We do not collect account registration information because LizSpeedTest does not require an account.

7. Data retention and deletion

Local history stays on your device until you remove it in the app or uninstall the app. App settings are also stored locally and are removed when the app is uninstalled.

Android device backup features may copy local app data to your own Google account backup, depending on your system settings.

8. Children's privacy

LizSpeedTest is a general-purpose network utility and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page.
