2. Description of the App

LizSpeedTest is a free Android application that allows users to measure their internet connection speed, including download speed, upload speed, ping (latency), jitter, and packet loss. The App connects to a global network of distributed speed test servers to conduct measurements.

Key features include:

  • Real-time download and upload speed measurement
  • Ping, jitter, and packet loss measurement
  • Automatic or manual server selection from a global network of speed test servers
  • Local test history (up to 300 records, stored on your device only)
  • Multi-language support
  • Customizable speed units and connection modes
